The Golden Chapter
Adults 55+ · Venous Insufficiency
19–20% of Americans over 50 experience chronic peripheral edema — the single largest affected population.
  • Shoes fit in the morning, won't fit by afternoon
  • Legs that feel heavy and exhausting by midday
  • Medical advice that amounts to "just elevate your feet"
02
The Turning Point
Weight-Related Swelling
Excess weight compresses veins and is a primary driver of venous insufficiency — one of the most supplement-receptive demographics online.
  • Socks and shoes leaving visible marks by end of day
  • Swelling that discourages movement — worsening the cycle
  • Skepticism built from products that didn't deliver
03
The New Chapter
Pregnancy · Hormonal Cycles
80% of pregnant women experience edema. Hormonal swelling affects millions more monthly — a high-intent audience actively seeking safe, natural support.
  • Puffy feet and ankles that make daily movement difficult
  • Monthly swelling cycles they can predict but can't stop
  • Skepticism toward anything pharmaceutical
04
The On-Your-Feet Crew
Nurses · Teachers · Retail · Desk Workers
Prolonged standing and sitting are among the most consistent clinical triggers for peripheral edema — a massive, underserved TikTok audience.
  • Dead-leg feeling after a long shift
  • Travel and desk days leaving feet swollen
  • Too busy for complex routines — want something simple
05
Side Effect Survivors
Medication-Induced Swelling
Amlodipine, prednisone, NSAIDs, and hormonal drugs all commonly cause fluid retention. Millions feel trapped between needing their medication and hating the side effects.
  • Need the medication — but hate what it does
  • Swelling that appeared exactly when the prescription started
  • Providers who acknowledge it but offer no solution
